About Us

Welcome to White Ribon Renal Care. We are dedicated to providing high quality care to individuals who are living with kidney disease. Our center is staffed with a team of experienced healthcare professionals who are committed to delivering compassionate care and treatment to our patients.

Our Services

Dialysis treatment: Our center provides state-of-the-art dialysis treatment to patients with end stage renal disease. We offer both hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis options to meet the unique needs of each patient. Medical Care: We provide comprehensive medical care to our patients, including regular check-ups, and other treatments as necessary. Nutritional Counseling: Our experienced dietitians offer personalized nutritional counseling to help patients manage their kidney disease and maintain a healthy diet.

Why Choose Us

Experienced Staff: Our center is staffed with experienced healthcare professionals who have extensive experience in the field of nephrology. State-of-the-Art-Facility: Our center is equipped with the latest technology and equipment to ensure the best possible care for our patients. Personalized Care: We understand that every patient is unique, and we strive to provide personalized care that means the individual needs of each patient. Convenient Location: Our center is located in a convenient location with ample parking, making it easyfor patients to access our services.

Contact Us

If you or a loved one is in need of dialysis or other renal services, please don't hesitate to contact us. Our staff is available to answer any questions you may have and help you schedule an appointment.
